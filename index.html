Our Mission

At MIC Women's Badminton Club, we are dedicated to promoting the sport of badminton and fostering a vibrant and inclusive community of women players.

Our Vision

Our vision is to bring out women to give themselves the much needed time for themselves to socialize and be physically active and meet like-minded individuals.

Our Platform

Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or a seasoned player seeking to refine your skills, our club offers a platform for players of all levels seeking to improve their game.

About Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport that is played indoors. It's a fantastic way to improve physical fitness, agility, and hand-eye coordination while having a great time with friends or fellow enthusiasts. Originating from ancient Indian roots, badminton has grown to become a globally popular sport, known for its thrilling rallies and competitive spirit.
